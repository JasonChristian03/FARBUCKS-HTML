About Us

FARBUCKS is an Indonesian coffee shop that serve the best quality coffee. Founded in the year 2022 by Bambang and Pardede, the team has been developing their own style of coffee since 2019 to brew the best coffee in town.

Our first store was located in Tangerang, where we started our journey before we gained popularity around Indonesia. After a few years, we started to expand around Indonesia, because we believe that everyone would enjoy our coffee so we kept on developing our brewing skills. We hope that everyone could enjoy and be satisfied by our coffee.
